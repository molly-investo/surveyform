About us

Akili na Roho is a programme that was founded in 2010 by a group of psychologists whose main focus was the frequent occurrences of conflicts in society. The present society has become very individualistic yet people cannot handle their problems without help whic is not easily given and hence this organization.

Community for the people

There are only a number of psychologists, hence unproportional to the population but thats where you come in. What we are building is a community for the peole which is made of you and me. We train volunteeers on how to handle and help the ailing generation since the first step to empowerment is dealing with the ignorance in that field. In short, we need in order to prove helpful to the community.
